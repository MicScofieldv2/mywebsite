Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install your products?

After purchase, you'll receive a download link and detailed installation instructions via email. Our launcher makes the process simple - just run the installer, log in with your account credentials, and follow the on-screen instructions. If you encounter any issues, our 24/7 support team is available to assist you.

Are your products safe to use?

Yes, we prioritize security above all else. Our products use advanced encryption and protection methods to ensure they remain undetected. We constantly update our software to stay ahead of anti-cheat systems, and our status page provides real-time information about detection risks. Our 99% security rate is a testament to our commitment to keeping your accounts safe.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods including major credit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Litecoin). Cryptocurrency payments offer an additional layer of privacy and come with a 5% discount on all purchases.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 24-hour money-back guarantee if our product doesn't work as described. However, refunds are not available if a product becomes detected after purchase, as this is beyond our control. We recommend checking our status page regularly for the most up-to-date information on our products.

How often do you update your products?

We update our products regularly to ensure optimal performance and security. Major updates typically occur weekly, with minor patches and security updates released as needed. Lifetime subscribers receive all updates for free, while subscription-based users get updates for the duration of their subscription period.
